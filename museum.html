Current Exhibits

Four chambers of wonder, thousands of stories.

Chamber I

The Golden Age

Featuring original escape gear used by Harry Houdini during his legendary 1912 world tour. See the 'Water Torture Cell' keys and the 'Handcuff King's' personal ledger.

Chamber II

Arcane Artifacts

Step back into the 16th century. Our collection includes original alchemical tomes, medieval sleight-of-hand tools, and the mysterious 'Oracle Bowl' of Delphi.

Chamber III

Mechanical Magic

Discover the clockwork wonders of the 18th century. Witness the 'Chess-Playing Turk' and the self-writing 'Boy Automaton' in a rare live demonstration.

Chamber IV

The Future of Illusion

From digital misdirection to aerospace-grade levitation rigs. See the evolution of magic through the lens of modern technology and psychology.
